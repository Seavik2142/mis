Table Explorer

users Identity

User accounts, roles (is_admin), and hashed credentials.

products Catalog

Item master list, prices, SKUs, and inventory stock balances.

customers Profiles

Purchaser details, companies, and segments (VIP, Active).

orders Sales

Header sales transactions, totals, and shipment statuses.

order_items Sales Line

Quantities and prices bought per product in an order.

inventory_movements Audit Ledger

Chronological changes to product inventory with audit notes.
